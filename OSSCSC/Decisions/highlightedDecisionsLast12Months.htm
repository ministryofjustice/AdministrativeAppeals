Decisions highlighted in the last 12 months

The Editorial Board of the Upper Tribunal (Administrative Appeals Chamber) regularly highlights decisions of London and Edinburgh Upper Tribunal judges and Northern Ireland Commissioners which are considered to be of particular interest or importance.

We welcome comments from users as to the suitability of decisions for reporting. If you think that any of the highlighted decisions or any other recent Judge's decision merits reporting, please contact Miss J Walker, Secretary to the Editorial Board Jill Walker giving your reasons.

The Editorial Board has set out criteria for reporting in the paper 'Guidelines for Reporting' which we hope you may find helpful.
