HM Courts & Tribunals Service (HMCTS) with effect from 1 April 2011

Highlighted decisions December 2010

Great Britain and Northern Ireland decisions highlighted by the Editorial Board in December 2010

Armed forces compensation scheme

SSSD v AW (AFCS) [2010] UKUT 317 (AAC)
(CAF/609/2009)
Whether the results of appropriate medical treatment which may not be taken into account as part of the initiating injury may be taken into account if the treatment gives long-term problems not covered in the descriptor.

Child Support

DW v CMEC (CSM) [2010] UKUT 196 (AAC)
(CCS/648/2009)
Variation – diversion of income into a pension fund – regulation 19(4) of the Child Support (Variations) Regulations 2000 – interaction between child support provisions and income tax provisions for contributions to pension funds – paragraph 5 of the Schedule to the Child Support (Maintenance Calculation and Special Cases) Regulations 2000 –previous conflicting decisions CCS/2707/2007 and CCS/289/2008 – income tax law requirements discussed – CCS/2707/2007 followed subject to additional consideration of the income tax position.

Criminal injuries compensation

SS v CIA & FTT (CIC) [2010] UKUT 410 (AAC)
(JR/126/2010)
Awards for mental injury –differences between 1996 scheme and 2001 scheme – meaning of "change in medical condition" in test for re-opening a case.

Disability discrimination in schools

AS v Buckinghamshire CC [2010] UKUT 407 (AAC)
(HS/754/2009)
First-tier Tribunal has no jurisdiction to consider claims in respect of an "admissions decision" (DDA 1995 s28K & s94 of School Standards & Framework Act 1998).

Income support

SA v SSWP (IS) [2010] UKUT 345 (AAC)
CIS/544/2010
Whether an educational establishment may also be a "care home".

Information rights

Smartsource v IC & a group of 19 additional parties [2010] UKUT 415 (AAC)
(GIA/2458/2010)
Environmental information – whether a privatised water company is a "public authority" for the purposes of the Environmental Information Regulations (2004 (SI 2004/3391).

Maternity benefits

AB v SSWP (MA) [2010] UKUT 287 (AAC)
(CSG/192/2010)
Operation of UK and EU law in relation to a claimant who was entitled to maternity allowance and was living in Poland.

AM v SSWP (SF) [2010] UKUT 344 (AAC)
(CCIS/2343/2009)
Child tax credit "payable at a rate higher than the family element" means paid at a level not including any individual child element, even if above the family element due to rounding.

Special Educational Needs

Bury MBC v SU (SEN) [2010] UKUT 406 (AAC)
(HS/1350/2010)
Special educational provision – proper construction of section 316 of the Education Act 1996 .

EH v KCC (SEN) [2010] UKUT 376 (AAC)
(S/3030/2009)
Whether the marginal cost to the public purse of providing education is relevant.

Tax Credits

CM v SSWP (TC) [2010] UKUT 400 (AAC)
(CSTC/299/2010)
Responsible for child and child care credits – proper application of "main responsibility" test.

Tribunal procedure and practice

AP-H v SSWP (DLA) [2010] UKUT 183 (AAC)
(CDLA/3182/2009)
Exercise of discretion to investigate issue not raised by the parties.

Carmarthenshire CC v MW (SEN) [2010] UKUT 348 (AAC)
(HSW/2001/2010)
Solid grounds are required for a suspension of the First-tier Tribunal’s decision – suspension granted to minimise possible disruption to pupil.

CB v Suffolk CC (Enforcement reference) [2010] UKUT 413 (AAC)
(HS/1371/2010)
Enforcement – compliance with rule 16 – how to challenge a witness summons – guidance on witness summons – contempt of court – penalty.

FC v Suffolk CC (SEN) [2010] UKUT 368 (AAC)
(HS/665/2010)
Barring a respondent is appropriate only in cases of blatant disregard of directions – new evidence not admissible – First-tier Tribunal entitled to use its own expertise and UT should give it a margin of appreciation.

SSWP v AM (IS) [2010] UKUT 428 (AAC)
(CIS/384/2010)
Whether the tribunal which hears the appeal against the subsequent recovery decision is bound by the decision of the previous tribunal that the claimant is not "living together as husband and wife".
