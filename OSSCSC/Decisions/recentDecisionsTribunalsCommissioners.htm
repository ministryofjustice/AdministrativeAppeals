Decisions of Three–Judge Panels in the last 12 months

The following cases have been decided by a Three–Judge Panel, which has the same status as the former Tribunals of Commissioners. See paragraph 37 of Dorset Healthcare Trust v MH [2009] UKUT 4 (AAC) for current guidance on that status.

Right to reside – worker status

Secretary of State for Work and Pensions v FE [2009] UKUT 287, 18.12.09
Income support – right to reside – whether possible to retain "worker" status under Article 7.3(c) of Directive 2004/38/EC in the context of an income support claim

Enforcement powers of Upper Tribunal

PA v CMEC [2009] UKUT 283 (AAC), 10.12.09
MR v CMEC [2009] UKUT 284 (AAC), 10.12.09
MR v CMEC [2009] UKUT 285 (AAC), 10.12.09
Reference by First–tier Tribunal – powers of UT under section 25 of the Tribunals Courts and Enforcement Act 2007 in relation to a failure by a party (in each of these cases, the non-resident parent) to produce specified documents

Membership of a religious community

Secretary of State for Work and Pensions v IS [2009] UKUT 200 AAC, 08.10.09
Whether barring entitlement to income-related benefits on grounds of "membership of a religious community" is discriminatory contrary to Article 14 of the European Convention on Human Rights taken with Article 9).
