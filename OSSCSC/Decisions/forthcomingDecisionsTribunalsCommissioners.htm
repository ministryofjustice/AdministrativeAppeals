Forthcoming decisions of Three–Judge Panels

The following cases will be decided by Three-Judge Panels, which have the same status as the former Tribunals of Commissioners. See Dorset Healthcare Trust v MH [2009] UKUT 4 (AAC) at paragraph 37 for current guidance.

Criminal injuries compensation

JR/1510/2009 (HW)
Whether “a reckless assault”, is a crime and, if so, whether it is necessarily a crime of violence

JR/1860/2009 (GJ)
Whether suicide on a road may involve a crime of violence

JR/1739/2009 (CB)
Whether making a false claim of rape amounts to a crime of violence

An oral hearing is to be held on 24.05.10

DLA and residential care

CDLA/1340/2009
Payability of DLA while claimant in publicly funded residential care; agreement between claimant and LA re deferral of payment of self-funded fees pending sale of house; whether DLA entitlement can only restart from date the existence of agreement was notified to the DWP
An oral hearing is to be held on 11.05.10

Recovery of overpayment – computer interface

CG/2739/2009
