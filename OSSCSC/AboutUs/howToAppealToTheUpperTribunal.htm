HM Courts & Tribunals Service (HMCTS) with effect from 1 April 2011

On 1 April 2011 the Tribunals Service (TS) merged with HM Courts Service (HMCS) to form HM Courts & Tribunals Service (HMCTS). As a result of this, the webpage that you are looking for has been moved.

Please follow one of the links below to find information about Tribunals.

Please visit this website if your search is for corporate information. Tribunals’ forms and guidance can also be found here.
